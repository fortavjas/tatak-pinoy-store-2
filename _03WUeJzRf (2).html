Our Story

Everything Filipino, under one roof.

We opened our doors on Joyce Street with one simple goal: to bring the taste of home a little closer. From the snacks you grew up with to the rice and sauces that make every meal feel right, our shelves are stocked with the real, authentic brands you've been missing.

Step inside and you'll find friendly faces who speak your language, a warm market-stall welcome, and that unmistakable Pinoy hospitality. Whether you're shopping for the week or sending love back home, you're part of the family here.
